My Expericence

Researcher & Research Scientist

Ghent University (Belgium) & International Institute of Tropical Agriculture (Kenya, Nigeria) | 2018 - 2023

Conducted various experiments to manage plant-parasitic nematodes, including greenhouse, microplot, and field trials.

Worked on integrative taxonomy and molecular phylogeny of plant-parasitic nematodes, focusing on linking species with molecular barcodes.

Maintained and multiplied reference populations of plant-parasitic nematodes in vitro & in vivo for use in research projects.

Investigated the impact of plant-parasitic nematodes on plant health and productivity, including screening crop germplasm to identify resistant material.

Mentored junior researchers and provided technical support in the Lab, improving productivity by 60%.

Developed a rapid screening approach for nematodes, reducing screening time by 30% and increasing efficiency by 50%.

Collaborated with a global team of scientists and technicians to successfully complete multiple projects on time and within budget.

Facilitated a training on molecular plant-parasitic nematode diagnostic workshop funded by the EU in Kenya, attracting 20 attendees, with positive feedback from 95% of participants.
